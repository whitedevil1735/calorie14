Push Ups

Primary Muscles worked: Chest, shoulders, triceps

Start in a solid plank position with your hands aligned directly under your shoulders and elbows
Lower your chest toward the floor in a controlled motion by bending your elbows, keeping them close to your body
Avoid letting your arms flare out. Then, drive through your palms to push yourself back up quickly to the starting position. Repeat

Crunches

Primary Muscles Worked: Upper abs

Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor
Place your hands behind your head, engage your core, and lift your shoulders off the ground
Pause briefly, then lower back down slowly. Avoid pulling on your neck. Repeat.

High knee

Primary Muscles Worked: Quads, calves, hip flexors, core

Stand tall with feet hip-width apart and arms at your sides
Quickly drive one knee up toward your chest while pumping the opposite arm
Alternate knees at a fast pace, landing softly on the balls of your feet. Maintain an upright posture. Repeat.

Mountain climbers

Primary Muscles Worked: Core, shoulders, quads

Start in a plank position with your hands under shoulders
Drive one knee toward your chest, then quickly switch legs
Continue alternating legs at a fast pace while keeping your hips low and core engaged. Repeat.

Stationary lunges

Primary Muscles Worked: Quads, hamstrings, glutes

Stand tall and step one foot forward into a lunge
Lower your back knee toward the floor while keeping your front knee over the ankle
Push through your front heel to return to the start. Switch legs. Repeat.

Cross crunches

Primary Muscles Worked: Obliques, upper abs

Lie on your back with knees bent and hands behind your head
Lift your shoulder and twist your torso to bring one elbow toward the opposite knee
Lower down and alternate sides. Keep the motion controlled. Repeat.

Start standing, then squat and place hands on the ground

Explode up into a jump. Land softly and repeat.

Plank

Primary Muscles Worked: Core, shoulders, glutes

Place forearms on the ground with elbows under shoulders
Extend legs behind you and keep your body in a straight line

Sideplank

Primary Muscles Worked: Obliques, shoulders, glutes

Lie on your side and prop yourself up on one forearm
Stack your feet and lift your hips off the ground to form a straight line
Hold this position, keeping your core tight. Switch sides. Repeat.

Russiantwists

Primary Muscles Worked: Obliques, core

Sit on the floor with knees bent and feet slightly lifted
Lean back slightly and clasp your hands together
Twist your torso side to side, touching the ground beside your hips each time. Repeat.

Squats

Primary Muscles Worked: Quads, hamstrings, glutes

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly out
Push your hips back and bend your knees to lower down
Go as low as comfortable, then drive through your heels to stand. Repeat.
