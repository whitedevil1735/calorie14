    Healthy Diet

    A healthy diet provides essential nutrients to your body, promoting overall health and reducing the risk of chronic diseases. Here are some key components:

    • Fruits and Vegetables: Rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber.
    • Whole Grains: Oats, quinoa, and brown rice support digestion and energy levels.
    • Lean Proteins: Chicken, fish, legumes, and tofu help with muscle growth and repair.
    • Healthy Fats: Sources like avocado, olive oil, and nuts improve heart and brain health.
    • Hydration: Drinking plenty of water to stay hydrated and support bodily functions.
    • Limited Processed Foods: Reducing processed and sugary snacks for better overall health.

    Unhealthy Diet

    An unhealthy diet is typically high in calories, added sugars, unhealthy fats, and processed foods. Here are the key characteristics:

    • Excessive Refined Sugars: Found in sweets, sodas, and many packaged snacks.
    • High in Trans and Saturated Fats: Fried foods, fast food, and packaged snacks can harm heart health.
    • Overprocessed Foods: White bread, sugary cereals, and pre-packaged meals.
    • Low in Nutrients: Lack of fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
    • Excessive Salt: High-sodium foods like canned soups and salty snacks.
